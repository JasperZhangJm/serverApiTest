How to configure the network?


respons:
code:0
Thank you for reaching out to Aosu Technical Support! To configure the network for your Aosu security camera, follow these steps:

1. Check the Wi-Fi frequency: Make sure your router is set to 2.4GHz. Only C2E and C4L models support 5GHz. If needed, split your WiFi into two channels to support the camera.

2. Ensure the camera is in pairing mode: Press and hold the RESET button until the blue light flashes.

3. Check router stability: Confirm other devices can connect to the internet from the same network.

4. Verify distance between camera and router: Ensure the camera is within range for a strong signal. You may need to move devices closer or use a WiFi extender.

5. If issues persist, consider switching to the mobile phone's hotspot connection for setup.

If you encounter any further difficulties, please feel free to reach out to us for more assistance. -Aosu Team

question:
The battery of the wireless camera kit is draining too fast


respons:
code:0
In the wireless camera kit, the battery life after one charge is designed to last several months under normal use. However, there are various environmental factors at your home that may affect the battery life. Some factors that could lead to faster battery drain include:

1. Local weather: Charging efficiency of lithium-ion batteries is lower at temperatures below 40¬∞F / 4¬∞C, leading to more frequent charging when temperatures are lower.
2. Intense activities: If the camera faces high traffic areas triggering frequent video recording, it can reduce battery life.
3. Detection sensitivity: Adjusting the sensitivity settings leading to more events being monitored can also impact battery life.
4. Continuous live video viewing or prolonged communication through the camera can drain the battery faster.

To improve battery life:
- Ensure the camera is not facing areas with intense activity.
- Adjust detection sensitivity settings.
- Avoid prolonged live view or communication sessions.
- Keep the camera updated with the latest firmware.
- Monitor the number of events detected daily.

If the issue persists, please provide more details for further assistance.

Notification Settings


respons:
code:0
The message notification modes are divided into fast notification mode and smart notification mode. You can switch between these modes in "Settings - Notification Settings - Message Notification Mode".

Fast notification mode:
- When an event is detected, a message notification is sent immediately.
- No event type analysis is performed when the notification is sent. Additional event information can be viewed in the event playback.
- Suitable for users who need to quickly know the situation at the door.

Smart notification mode:
- Sends a message notification after the event recording is complete.
- Performs AI intelligent algorithm analysis on complete events and provides more accurate event types.
- Ideal for users who want precise information.

You can adjust these settings in the "Settings - Notification Settings" menu.
